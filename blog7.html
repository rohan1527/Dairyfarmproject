Benefits of Desi Ghee during Pregnancy

Ghee is one of the healthy foods your mom, grandmother, or aunt needs during pregnancy to consume.

Ghee, the Indian word of clarified butter, is a great source of fat and is often included in a pregnant woman's diet. But is ghee good for health during pregnancy?

Yes, when you're pregnant, eating a moderate amount of ghee is healthy every day. Ghee is easier to digest and improves metabolism, unlike other dairy products. Ghee is often used as a supplement for butter and oil because it is a good source of fat.

It is known fact that during pregnancy, balanced diet and healthy food is important. Also fats in ghee is an integral part of a balanced diet and can be included in a pregnant woman's diet.

During childbirth, you can add milk with ghee. Before adding too much ghee to your diet, however, check with your doctor. Ghee contains fair amounts of fatty amino acids, antioxidants, vitamins and minerals.
