Poduct details

Vermiwash is a liquid extract made from vermicompost that contains nutrients, enzymes, and beneficial microorganisms that are good for plants and soil. Vermiwash is made by allowing water to pass through tunnels made by earthworms. These tunnels are called drilospheres and are rich in bacteria.
Description

• Vermiwash is a natural growth supplement that can help plants grow faster and develop healthier roots.

• Vermiwash can improve soil texture, aeration, and tilth. It can also reduce soil compaction and increase the soil's water holding capacity.

• Vermiwash can help suppress plant diseases and kill pests.

• It contains decomposer bacteria and fungi that produce metabolites that prevent the growth of pathogenic bacteria and plant pests.

• Vermiwash can be used in sustainable agriculture practices to help plants grow in nutrition-depleted soils.

• You can apply vermiwash to plants using a watering can, spray bottle, or drip irrigation system.
